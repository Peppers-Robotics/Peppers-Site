About Us

We are the Peppers team from the Theoretical High School of Computer Science "Grigore Moisil" in Iași, a large family of students and alumni passionate about robotics, consisting this season of 15 members, 9 volunteers and peer-mentors.

Our goal is to familiarize the general public with the multidisciplinary STEM concept. During the 7 years since the team was founded, over 120 dedicated, competitive and generous young people have defined the team's spirit and its remarkable performances at the world's largest robotics competition for high school students, FIRST Tech Challenge.
Our Mission

STEM is an acronym for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, representing a group of interconnected academic and professional fields focused on innovation, problem-solving, and understanding the world through research, design, and application. It's a popular educational approach that integrates these subjects to develop critical thinking, digital literacy, and practical skills for the modern world.

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global non-profit that runs youth robotics competitions (like FIRST Robotics Competition) to inspire STEM, while robotics itself is an interdisciplinary field of engineering and computer science focused on designing, building, and using robots to perform tasks, combining AI, mechanics, and programming to create automated systems that can substitute or assist humans

CLANK

Matches run on a typical 5- to 12-minute cycle time per field, which consists of pre-match setup, a 30- second auto period, an 8-second transition period between auto and teleop, and a 2-minute teleop period, followed by the post-match reset.

During the match, robots collect artifacts and score them into their goal to classify and create the randomly selected motif. Robots can then open their gate to continue classifying additional artifacts. Artifacts which do not drop into the ramp will count as overflow. robots conclude the match by returning to their base.
